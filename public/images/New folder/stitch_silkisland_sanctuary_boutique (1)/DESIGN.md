# Amber & Onyx: A Refined Dark System

This design system establishes a high-contrast, luxurious aesthetic that balances classic elegance with modern softness. Built on a foundation of deep neutrals and vibrant metallic accents, the system is designed for premium digital experiences that require both presence and approachability.

## Visual Narrative
The interface transitions from a sharp, editorial feel to a more welcoming and fluid experience. By utilizing maximum roundedness, the system softens the intensity of the dark color palette, creating a modern "glassmorphism" or "organic luxury" vibe. The contrast between the serif headlines and the rounded UI elements suggests a brand that is established yet forward-thinking.

## Color Palette
The color strategy uses a "fidelity" approach to maintain rich, saturated tones even in a dark environment.
- **Primary Color:** #deb429 (Lustrous Amber) – Used for primary actions and key brand moments.
- **Secondary Color:** #d05329 (Burnt Clay) – Provides a warm, earthy counterpoint to the gold.
- **Tertiary Color:** #795536 (Deep Bark) – A muted brown used for subtle accents and decorative depth.
- **Neutral Base:** #0A0A0A (Onyx Black) – A near-pure black that provides an infinite depth for foreground elements.

## Typography
The system employs a sophisticated dual-font strategy:
- **Headlines:** Noto Serif – Adds a sense of authority, tradition, and timelessness.
- **Body & Labels:** Manrope – A geometric sans-serif that ensures high readability and a clean, technical edge in functional areas.

## Shape & Space
- **Roundedness (Level 3):** All interactive components, containers, and buttons utilize maximum corner radii (pill-shaped). This removes visual tension and makes the interface feel tactile and modern.
- **Spacing (Level 2):** The layout follows a balanced, standard density. It provides enough breathing room to feel premium without sacrificing information density or requiring excessive scrolling.

## Design Principles
1. **Luminous Depth:** Use the primary amber color to guide the eye through the deep onyx background.
2. **Organic Softness:** Every corner is rounded to create a cohesive, friendly environment despite the dark tone.
3. **Typographic Hierarchy:** Leverage the serif headline to announce sections with gravity, while keeping the utility of Manrope for interaction.

3. What Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you use our website:

  • Identity Data: Name, email address
  • Contact Data: Phone number, delivery address, postal code, city
  • Transaction Data: Order history, payment method (we do NOT store full card details)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies
  • Communication Data: Reviews, messages sent to us

4. How We Collect Your Data

We collect data through:

  • Account registration and order placement
  • Contact forms and email communication
  • Cookies and analytics tools (Google Analytics)
  • TikTok and Meta Pixel (for marketing)

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for:

  • ✅ Processing and delivering your orders
  • ✅ Sending order confirmations and updates
  • ✅ Customer support and responding to inquiries
  • ✅ Improving our website and services
  • ✅ Marketing (with your consent)
  • ✅ Legal compliance (tax invoices, accounting)

6. Data Sharing

We share your data only with:

  • Courier services: Fan Courier, SameDay (for delivery)
  • Payment processors: Stripe (for card payments)
  • Email service: Resend (for order confirmations)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (website traffic)

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

7. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on:

  • Supabase (cloud database, encrypted)
  • Cloudflare infrastructure (secure servers)

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for:

  • Order data: 10 years (legal requirement for tax purposes)
  • Account data: Until account deletion request
  • Marketing consent: Until withdrawal

9. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in (session cookies)
  • Remember your language preference
  • Track website analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Enable marketing pixels (TikTok, Meta)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.

10. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • 🔍 Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • ✏️ Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • 🗑️ Erasure: Request deletion of your data

  • 📤 Portability: Receive your data in a readable format
  • ❌ Object: Object to processing for marketing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@Silkisland.ro. We will respond within 30 days.

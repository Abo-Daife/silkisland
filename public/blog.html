Lingerie Care

How to Care for Silk Lingerie: A Complete Guide

April 23, 2026 • 4 min read

Silk is one of the most delicate and luxurious fabrics in the world. Proper care ensures your silk pieces remain beautiful for years. Learn the essential steps for washing, drying, and storing your silk lingerie to maintain its elegance and softness.

Key Tips:

🧼 Always hand wash silk in cold water with mild detergent.

🌬️ Never wring or twist silk—gently press out excess water with a towel.

☀️ Avoid direct sunlight when drying to prevent color fading.

📦 Store flat or rolled, never on hangers which can stretch the fabric.

Finding Your Perfect Bra Size at Home

April 20, 2026 • 5 min read

Studies show that 80% of women wear the wrong bra size. An ill-fitting bra can cause discomfort, poor posture, and affect how your clothes look. Follow our simple guide to measure yourself correctly at home.

How to Measure:

📏 Band Size: Measure directly under your bust. Round to the nearest whole number.

📐 Bust Size: Measure around the fullest part of your bust.

🔢 Calculate: Subtract band from bust. Each inch difference = one cup size.

✅ Try before buying: Always check our size guide when shopping at SilkIsland.
